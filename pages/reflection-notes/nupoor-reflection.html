Nupoor Mahajan's Reflection

Prepared by: Nupoor Mahajan

I worked on the Tokenization, Stemming, and Bag of Words concept cards, the Stemming vs Lemmatization comparison, and the Complete Text Preprocessing Pipeline for this repository. Going into this project, I understood these ideas only at a surface level - I knew tokenization "split text into words," but building the actual concept pages made me think much harder about edge cases, like how a simple space-based tokenizer completely breaks down on contractions or on languages without clear word boundaries.

The concept that became clearest to me while working on this was the difference between stemming and lemmatization. I had always used them somewhat interchangeably in my head, but writing the comparison page forced me to actually trace through examples by hand - seeing "studies" become the non-word "studi" under stemming versus the correct "study" under lemmatization made the trade-off between speed and accuracy click in a way that just reading definitions never did.

The biggest challenge was designing the preprocessing pipeline diagram in a way that was accurate but still easy for a first-time reader to follow. I had to resist the urge to cram in every possible detail and instead focus on the core sequence of steps.

Overall, this project pushed me to move from "I've heard of this term" to "I can explain this term to someone else with a proper example," which I think is the real test of whether you've actually learned something.

- Nupoor Mahajan
