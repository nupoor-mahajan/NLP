Hansa Gusaiwal's Reflection

Prepared by: Hansa Gusaiwal

My contributions to this repository included the Lemmatization, TF-IDF, and Word Embeddings concept cards, the TF vs TF-IDF comparison, and the Feature Engineering Pipeline. Before this project, TF-IDF was a formula I could recite but not fully explain - I could tell you it involved "term frequency" and "inverse document frequency," but I couldn't clearly say why multiplying the two actually produces something more useful than either one alone.

Writing the TF-IDF concept page and comparison changed that. Working through a concrete example - where a common word like "the" gets a low score while a rare, meaningful word gets a high one - made the purpose of the formula finally make sense to me, rather than it just being something to memorise for an exam.

The most challenging part for me was explaining Word Embeddings in a way that felt beginner-friendly without oversimplifying it into something incorrect. It took a few rewrites to find a way to describe why "good" and "great" end up close together in vector space without diving straight into heavy linear algebra.

Building the Feature Engineering Pipeline also helped me see how TF-IDF isn't a standalone trick - it fits into a larger sequence starting from clean tokens and ending in a numeric vector a model can actually use. This project made these Week 1 basics feel far more connected and practical than they did in isolation.

- Hansa Gusaiwal
